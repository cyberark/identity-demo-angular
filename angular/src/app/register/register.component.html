The purpose of multi-factor authentication (MFA) is to confirm a user's claimed identity by utilizing something they know (password) and a second factor other than something they have or something they are.

The CyberArk Identity API demo allows you to use SMS messages and email as a secondary authentication factor. By selecting this option, you agree to receive one-time access codes and links via SMS messages or email. Your wireless carrier's standard messaging rates apply to SMS correspondence.
